Chrome Ore Operations

Berkan-Fellmann Enterprises exports by rail about 3,000mt of lumpy chrome ore per month with an average grade of 40% Cr2O3.

The Material is primarily produced by artisanal and small-scale miners through a tributary agreement with whom we give support in the form of hiring and maintenance of equipment, fuel facility support and covering other various overheads.

Another significant source of this export cargo is consolidation of material produced by small-scale miners who have parcels to avail but may not have the sufficient mass to warrant an export.

Our Business model ensures that there is upside to the smaller miners who face the toughest prices during price fluctuations.

Agricultural Operations

Through years of experience within the agro-commodity trading industry, we have established a large network of personal contacts with trading companies, feed producers and mills in the agricultural business all over Southern Africa.

We have supplied large quantities of food stuffs, fertilizers and Non-GMO Maize for human consumption and it remain our desire, to be the sole leading supplier of agricultural commodities in the country.

BFE imports Grade A Non-GMO Maize for human consumption. This enhances the company’s ability to operate optimally in an increasingly complex environment and through its personal contacts in Africa, the company acquires the best grains and food stuffs.

Customer satisfactory is our main focus, our imports are packaged in a way that satisfies our customers and according to the requirements of each customer.  Through its 80% subsidiary of Farirai Quality Foods, the company has supplied grains and food stuffs to the manufacturing and milling industry and NGOs

Fuel and Lubricant Operations

Another area BFE specializes in is distribution and marketing of petroleum products throughout the Southern African region.

Our experienced team ensures that our products are delivered to our customers on time, within budget and with best quality. We are intensifying our focus in Zimbabwe and we are looking at ways of bringing fuel and lubricants at a cost-effective price to our local customers.

It is the company’s focus to provide customers with quality merchandise at competitive prices and a flexible sales and service network nationwide.

BFE specializes in the trading of fuel and lubricants as well as various commodities and mineral mining and trading.
