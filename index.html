About OPHTZ

Founded in 2002
We give hope to the hopeless and helpless

Operation Hope Trust Zimbabwe is a need-driven organization that exists to empower individuals, families and communities with regards to health, socio-economic status, community and disaster relief through humanitarian and developmental projects, ultimately ensuring an improved quality of life for its beneficiaries whilst offering sustainable solutions to their situations.

We are a need-driven and highly networked organisation, bringing relief, empowerment and sustainable solutions to its beneficiaries.
