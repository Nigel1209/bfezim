About Us

Berkan-Fellmann Enterprises (BFE) was established in 2011 with the purpose of mineral mining, mineral trading, agricultural and commodity trading, fuel trading and other forms of productive investments.

Our model involves mining Lumpy Chrome Ore in tribute arrangements along the Great Dyke.

Through its mineral resources’ division, the company acquires exportable minerals and facilitates sizeable parcels for the export market, by rail and road (8,000mt exports per month). This allows the company leverage in negotiating competitive transport rates on return loads for grains and food stuffs

BFE is involved in the trading of agricultural commodities. Our model includes importation of Grade A, Non-GMO maize for human consumption

Through its 80% subsidiary of Farirayi Quality Foods, the company has supplied grains and food stuffs to the manufacturing and milling industry and NGOs

Through its highly motivated team, BFE is also involved in the fuel and lubricants trading business where it seeks to bring fuel and lubricants at a cost-effective price to its local customers

BFE also has its own assets on which is continually mechanizing in order to increase output and achieve resource optimization from economies of scale.
